# **App Name**: PANETONE GOURMET DA MAY

## Core Features:

- Homepage Showcase: Display featured videos and premium content prominently using the card layout and styling specified.
- YouTube Video Integration: Embed YouTube videos within the app using a WebView or an integrated player, ensuring smooth playback.
- Bottom Navigation Menu: Provide easy navigation with a stylish bottom menu including Home, Videos, About, and Contact.
- About Section: Display detailed information about the content creator or brand.
- Database persistence: Store information such as video analytics.

## Style Guidelines:

- Background: Matte black (#0D0D0D) for a premium and aggressive feel.
- Highlights: Metallic gold (#D4AF37) for titles and premium buttons.
- Text: White (#FFFFFF) for high contrast and readability.
- Cards/Sections: Charcoal gray (#1A1A1A) for internal sections and cards.
- Titles: Strong, bold, and spaced font for a premium headline style.
- Subtitles: Medium weight, clear, and legible font.
- Body Text: Simple, modern sans-serif font.
- Buttons: Bold, uppercase font for high contrast and impact.
- Recommended font pairing: 'Belleza' (sans-serif) for headlines and 'Alegreya' (serif) for body text.
- Minimalist white icons for the bottom navigation menu.
- Clean layout with ample whitespace for a sophisticated look.
- Large cards with rounded edges and soft shadows.
- Symmetrical spacing throughout the app.
- Simple animation (slight darkening) on button press for a premium touch.
